Welcome to Atomic Addicts. Habits are essential to our health. They can make or break your chances of achieving and maintaining lifestyle goals such as sticking to an eating plan, exercising regularly, and managing finances, along with increasing quality of life and promoting longevity. This app is for tracking, managing and cultivating good habits. In-built are five key habits for you to choose from:
  • Sleeping
  • Exercise
  • Water Intake
  • Smoking
  • Savings

All the motivation in the world isn't going to help you if you don't have a system or a collection of good habits to lean on. Systems keep you in check. Conversely, all the motivation in the world won't help you beat bad habits either.

Remember, it ain’t about the cheddar. It’s about the cheese.
